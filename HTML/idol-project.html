Description

Early Life

Zhang Linghe was born in Wuxi, Jiangsu in 1997. In 2016, Zhang was admitted to Nanjing Normal University and majored in electrical engineering. During his time there, he joined the Aerospace Society because of his interest in physics. After meeting an agent, he became interested in acting.

Career

In 2020, he made his television debut in the costume romance drama (Maiden Holmes) and also starred in the youth romance drama (Sparkle Love).The following year, he appeared in the urban romance drama Love Crossed and the youth school drama (Flourish in Time). In 2022, he portrayed Chang Heng, the god of war in the costume fairy tale drama (Love Between Fairy and Devil). In 2023, he starred in the costume romance drama(Story of Kunning Palace) and historical drama( My Journey To You )
