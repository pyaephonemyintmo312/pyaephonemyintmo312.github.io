Skin Booster Treatment

Skin boosters are injectables that improve skin texture, elasticity, hydration and overall appearance. Skin booster treatment involves injecting hyaluronic acid which is a soft, gel-like substance under the skin, which improves the skinâ€™s elasticity, firmness and radiance giving an overall glow.

How it works
Skin boosters contain hyaluronic acid (HA), a natural substance that improves skin's elasticity and suppleness
The HA is injected into the skin via micro-injections
The treatment stimulates the body's own collagen and elastin production

Benefits
Improves skin's texture, elasticity, and hydration
Gives skin a glowing complexion
Improves the appearance of fine lines and wrinkles
Can be used to treat the face, neck, hands, and other areas

Types of skin boosters
Profhilo: An injectable skin booster made of stabilized hyaluronic acid
Restylane Skinboosters: A cross-linked hyaluronic acid treatment that can be used to treat the face, hands, neckline, and neck
Revs Pro 32: A skin booster that contains 32 essential vitamins, minerals, and amino acids Safety and downtime
Skin boosters are generally considered a safe treatment with little to no downtime.
