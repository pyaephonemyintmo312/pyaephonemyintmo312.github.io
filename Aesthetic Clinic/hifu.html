HIFU Treatment

High-intensity focused ultrasound (HIFU) is a minimally invasive procedure that uses sound waves to treat prostate cancer and other conditions.

How HIFU works -- A probe is inserted into the rectum The probe sends sound waves that heat up and destroy cancer cells The procedure is minimally invasive, with no incisions or needles

HIFU for prostate cancer-- HIFU can treat prostate cancer and pain from cancer that has spread to bones , HIFU can improve urinary and sexual function for some patients , HIFU can be used to treat the whole prostate or just areas of cancer

HIFU for skin tightening -- HIFU can tighten sagging skin on the neck, cheeks, and eyelids, HIFU can enhance jawline definition and smooth skin, HIFU can improve skin texture and firmness

Side effects-- Treated areas can become red and blotchy, especially for people with sensitive skin , The prostate can swell, so a catheter may be placed to drain the bladder --
