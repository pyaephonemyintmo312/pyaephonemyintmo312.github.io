IPL Skin Rejuvention Treatment

This heat energy causes collagen fibers to contract and produces new collagen The treated skin cells look more like younger skin cells .

Benefits -- Reduces redness and flushing from rosacea, Improves skin tone, Reduces fine lines and pore size, Diminishes acne, acne scarring, and skin discoloration, Reduces the appearance of sun damage and freckles

, Side effects -- Mild pain and redness during or after treatment,

Risks-- Incorrectly performed IPL treatments can cause serious injury, including: Burns, Permanent scarring, Eye damage, Pigmentation changes, Infection, Blistering, Increased hair growth, Treatment frequency

-- Most patients need at least three IPL sessions, spaced four weeks apart Skin improvements can last 6 months to 1 year IPL is not effective or safe for treating darker skin tones.
